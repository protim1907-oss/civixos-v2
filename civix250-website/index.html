How It Works

Three steps to civic impact

From signup to making your voice heard — in minutes.

01

Choose Your District

Sign up and select your congressional district. Civix250 instantly surfaces the issues, representatives, and discussions that matter to your community.

02

Post & Engage

Create posts about local issues, vote on what matters, comment on discussions, and send AI-assisted messages directly to your elected representatives.

03

Drive Real Change

Officials see aggregated sentiment, trending issues, and constituent priorities — enabling smarter, faster, and more accountable governance.
